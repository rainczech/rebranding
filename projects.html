Dallas Give Camp - Pro Bono Hackathon

An annual (COVID willing) event where developers donate their time, skill, and knowledge for various charities.

It is easy to underestimate the impact one can have as a developer to solve problems, encourage the sharing of information, and to make the world just a little bit better.

Imagine my surprise when Kermit and Miss Piggy promoted a charity in 2020 that I've supported through Dallas Give Camp by reworking their website over the past 2 years!

Dallas Give Camp Summary

I updated the websites for the Bermuda Turtle Project and Tour De Turtles, part of the Sea Turtle Conservancy. The design, structure, layout, and transferring of content from the older site was done mostly by myself.


WordPress was used so non-developers can update the information as necessary independently.
