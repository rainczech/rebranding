About Me

I am a Full Stack Engineer who has always had an interest in STEM. That interest has taken me from a Bachelor's degree in Psychology from Baylor University, to health care as a Lead Optician, and now, a Full Stack Engineer with a certification in Web Development from SMU. My background has instilled a strong sense of leadership, gumption, and teamwork.


I appreciate "paying it forward" by helping those who want to grow or change their careers in the technology field. I enjoy working with others as a Teaching Assistant, and speaking publically on various panels have been rewarding as well.


When I am not busy coding, I enjoy being active by hiking with my dogs, and spending time at the gym. As an amateur powerlifter, my competitive drive and discipline helped me win first place in my division at a meet.
