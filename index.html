Me In A Nutshell

I am a Full Stack Engineer who has always had an interest in STEM. That interest has taken me from a Bachelor's degree in Psychology from Baylor University, to health care as a Lead Optician, and now, Software Development Lead with a certification in Web Development from SMU. Having worked in healthcare with patients in both small and large office settings, I have developed strong sense of leadership, grit, and teamwork.


Changing my career was one of the hardest things that I have done. I am incredibly lucky to be able to do what I love. I embrace the concept of "paying it forward", and try to practice it by helping those who want to grow or change their careers in the technology field as well. I am a part time Teaching Assistant for the very school and bootcamp that trained me, and also participate in various panels as a speaker share my experiences.


I believe that learning and self-improvement is a lifelong process. When I am not working, I'm usually studying for some sort of certification to increase my depth of knowledge and to stay current. I also try to remain active in the tech community by attending conferences to continue my education and enhance my skill set.


To unwind, I enjoy being active by hiking with my dogs, spending time at the gym, or play Borderlands 3. In my former life, I was an amateur competitive powerlifter, and I still lift as a hobby and a way to stay healthy. Fun Fact: I won first place in my division at my first meet.
